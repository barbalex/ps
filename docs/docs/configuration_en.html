Configuration

Last updated: 6 May 2026

arten-fördern.app came about because:

  • there is no 'one size fits all' for nature conservation projects
  • and because very few public institutions or ecological consultancies can afford the development of a tailor-made product such as https://apflora.ch, https://vermehrung.ch or https://arteigenschaften.ch

That is why arten-fördern.app is highly configurable. This was for me the most demanding part of development. For you it means: you need to think at the very beginning about how to best organise your project and how to use arten-fördern.app for it.

For this, you have three options:

1 I'll just get started and deal with configuration when it becomes necessary

I'm glad if you take out a subscription for that. But there is a good chance things could have gone much better for you all. Retroactive configuration changes can be very time-consuming and affect everyone involved.

If a project is successful, it develops over the years. Later adjustments and extensions are therefore rarely avoidable even with good preparation. But they can be minimised.

2 I'll take a lot of time at the start and configure it myself

Good idea. Please let me know what I need to document better to simplify the process, or what should work differently for your case.

3 I'll take time at the start and get support

You can probably reduce your effort if I'm allowed to think along and advise. As well as the risk of having to adjust the configuration retroactively.

Your project and the optimal configuration of arten-fördern.app interests me. I am therefore willing at the start to collaborate at modest rates (free for ecological consultancies at the very beginning) and write off the rest as continuing education, networking and marketing.

I think it is fair that I offer this service for free at the beginning and raise the rate later: at the start, marketing and continuing education outweigh the rest for me. And you know the rate in advance.
