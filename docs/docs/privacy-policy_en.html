4. Definitions

What is personal data?

Personal data is any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person. This includes, for example, name, address, date of birth, email address or phone number, as well as IP address. Data about personal preferences such as leisure activities or memberships also counts as personal data.

What are special categories of personal data?

Special categories of personal data (also referred to as "sensitive data") include:

  • Data on religious, philosophical, political or trade union views or activities;
  • Data on health, private life, racial or ethnic origin, and sex life or sexual orientation;
  • Data relating to administrative or criminal proceedings and sanctions, as well as data on social welfare measures;
  • Genetic data and biometric data that uniquely identify a person.

Where required and where you disclose such data to us yourself, we may process data belonging to a special category of personal data. In that case, its processing is subject to stricter confidentiality. On arten-fördern.app, at most administrative measures (in the event of non-payment) would be conceivable. The other categories are not relevant.

What is the processing of personal data?

Processing is any handling of personal data, regardless of the means and methods used — in particular collecting, storing, retaining, using, modifying, disclosing, archiving, deleting or destroying personal data.

What is the disclosure of personal data?

This is the transmission or making accessible of personal data — for example publication or disclosure to a third party.

If you merely visit arten-fördern.app — that is, without registering or providing any other information — only the data that your browser automatically transmits to our server will be collected.

What data do we process?

In particular, the following data is processed to provide the web application and to create log files:

  • IP address
  • Technical information such as browser, operating system or screen resolution
  • The date and time of access

These data cannot be attributed to any specific person and are not merged with other data sources.

For what purpose do we process the data?

Log files are processed to ensure the functionality of the web application and to maintain the security of our information technology systems. In addition, we simply want to know how much interest there is in our web application.

To whom do we disclose the data?

Our disclosure of data is governed by our statements on data disclosure above.

How can you prevent data processing?

The data is only stored for as long as is necessary to achieve the purpose for which it was collected. Accordingly, data is deleted at the end of each session. The storage of log files is strictly necessary for the operation of the web application; you therefore have no means of objecting, unless you do not visit arten-fördern.app.

9.2 Cookies

Cookies are data stored on your device's operating system via the browser when you visit arten-fördern.app. When you log in, all your project data is synchronised to a local database in your browser — this probably also falls under the definition of cookies. Cookies do not cause any damage to your computer (other than using storage space) and do not contain viruses. The cookies we use are technically necessary for arten-fördern.app to function. Beyond that, we store nothing. Our cookies remain stored on your device until you delete them or they expire.

For what purpose do we process the data?

The data stored locally on your device serves the function for which you use arten-fördern.app. Beyond that, we store nothing — in particular no third-party cookies.

To whom do we disclose the data?

Our disclosure of data is governed by our statements on data disclosure above. The subsequent sections on individual data processing activities in this privacy policy should also be noted.

How can you prevent data processing?

Cookies are stored on your computer. You can delete them entirely, or disable or restrict their transmission by changing your browser settings. If cookies are disabled, arten-fördern.app will no longer work (only the homepage and the documentation).
