Quality Controls

Quality controls can help improve the quality of recorded data. Their purpose is to detect suspicious records.

Users with the role "Owner" or "Designer" can choose which of the over 100 ready-made quality controls to enable — and can create their own.

They are available at the project and sub-project level (species/biotope).

The result is lists of suspicious records that can be worked through one by one.

Quality controls have the advantage of not interrupting or hindering users during data entry. You can leave a form even if not all required fields have been filled in. But if you forget to follow up, a quality control will hopefully raise the alarm.

Quality controls should trigger as few false alarms as possible — ideally none at all under normal circumstances.
