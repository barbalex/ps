# Technical Requirements

_Last updated: 8 May 2026_

---

arten-fördern.app runs in the browser. Which one generally doesn't matter, but it should always be up to date.

arten-fördern.app works on any operating system.

As a [Progressive Web App](https://web.dev/explore/progressive-web-apps), you can also install arten-fördern.app: Open the app in the browser, after which the next step depends on the browser you are using. Often an 'install' prompt appears in the URL bar. Or there is a menu entry for it (in my case: 'Save and share' > 'Install page as app'). After installation you should find an entry in your PC's start menu and the app runs in its own window, without a URL bar.

You can then usually also launch arten-fördern.app from a shortcut. Depending on your operating system, it will generally behave like any app obtained from an app store or installed directly.

You don't need to worry about updates; they happen automatically.
